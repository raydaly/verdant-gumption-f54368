# Greatuncle: Content Strategy & Media Plan

This document outlines the philosophical and practical storytelling around Greatuncle, including blog series, video content, and archived research.

> [!NOTE]
> For the core application vision, onboarding philosophy, and technical guardrails, see the [Welcome Guide](WELCOME_GUIDE.md).

## Blog Post Series: The Greatuncle Philosophy

### Core Series (from Welcome Guide)
1. **How to be a "Greatuncle" for Your Extended Family**
   - *Focus*: Redefining the "Family Steward" as a modern digital guardian.
2. **Privacy as a Love Language: Why "No Cloud" Matters**
   - *Focus*: Why keeping data off the servers is the ultimate act of respect for your family.
3. **The Death of the Address Book (and How to Resurrect It)**
   - *Focus*: A cultural critique of why we lost our "source of truth" and how to get it back.
4. **Beyond the Notification: Making Birthdays Mean Something Again**
   - *Focus*: Moving from a "Facebook wall post" to a meaningful, intentional birthday ritual.
5. **Digital Minimalism for the Modern Family**
   - *Focus*: Why "one-purpose" apps are the secret to a calmer, more connected digital life.
6. **Fluid Families: Why We Ditched Folders for Smart Tags**
   - *Focus*: Explaining why the overlapping nature of families requires a tagging system, not buckets.
7. **Managing the "Other Side": The Kinkeeper's Dual Map**
   - *Focus*: How to use Greatuncle to manage both paternal and maternal sides in one clean interface.
8. **The Social Connectors: Embracing the Overlap**
   - *Focus*: Using the Overlap Matrix to find the "bridges" in your family tree.
9. **Subgroups: The Art of the @Cousins Tag**
   - *Focus*: How to create specific subsets of connection without cluttering the main list.
10. **Wishlists and Groceries: Stewardship in the Real World**
    - *Focus*: Transitioning from "knowing an address" to "sending a meal."
