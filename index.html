About 1.3

Grounded Connection

Greatuncle is a private, local-first tool designed to help you sustain meaningful connections. It is built for stability over noise—replacing the "viral" energy of social media with the steady, quiet work of staying rooted in your community.

Numbers and Friendships: Greatuncle separates your friendships into four categories based on how often you interact with them.

  • Inner: 5 people you interact with at least once a week.
  • Sympathy: 10 additional people with monthly interactions.
  • Acquaintances: 35 more people quarterly or semi-annually.
  • Active: 100 people you interact with less than once a year.

This gives you 150 people in your community.

Stability Over Signal: While social media thrives on "high-energy" spikes, grounded connection is about the slow, organic work of maintenance. Just as a tree needs deep roots, we need deep, sustained connections to stay resilient.

The Greatuncle Archetype: Inspired by the one who remembers every birthdate, knows the names of your neighbors, and checks in just to say hello. We ensure those who matter most never slip through the cracks of a busy life.

Stay rooted. Stay connected.
